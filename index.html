Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Wovenex may collect, use, store, and protect information when visitors interact with our website, communication channels, and digital services. By using our website or contacting us through our business channels, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Wovenex may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, submit an inquiry, or communicate with our team through the website or email. This may include your name, business name, email address, phone number, and any project or inquiry details you choose to share.

  • Contact information submitted through forms or email
  • Business and project-related details you provide voluntarily
  • Basic technical or usage information generated through website access

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by Wovenex may be used to respond to inquiries, communicate with potential clients, improve website performance, support business operations, and maintain a professional and secure digital experience. We use submitted information only for legitimate business and communication purposes.

  • Responding to questions and business inquiries
  • Providing service-related communication
  • Improving website usability and digital presentation
  • Maintaining internal records related to business discussions

3. Sharing of Information

Wovenex does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary for business operations, legal compliance, service delivery, security, or technical support. Any such sharing is limited to the extent required for those purposes.

4. Data Retention

We may retain submitted information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage business communication, maintain records, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, or support legitimate operational needs. Information no longer required may be deleted or securely removed when appropriate.

5. Data Protection

Wovenex applies reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, alteration, or loss. However, no website, email transmission, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights relating to the personal information you provide, including the right to request access, correction, update, or deletion of certain information. Requests may be submitted using the official Wovenex contact channel listed below.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website or communications may contain references or links to third-party websites, services, or tools. Wovenex is not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of third-party platforms. Users should review the policies of those third parties separately before engaging with them.

8. Policy Updates

Wovenex may update or revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect business, legal, technical, or operational changes. Any updated version will be published on the relevant page of our website and will become effective upon posting unless otherwise stated.
