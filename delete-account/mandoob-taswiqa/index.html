Mandoob Taswiqa Account Deletion

This page explains how delivery representatives can request deletion of their Mandoob Taswiqa account and associated information.

How to Request Account Deletion

Users may request permanent deletion of their account and associated data by contacting our support team through the official communication channels.

Required Information

  • Registered phone number.
  • Account holder name.
  • Any information necessary to verify account ownership.

Data Removal

Once the deletion request is verified and approved, account-related personal information will be removed or anonymized according to operational and legal requirements.

Support Contact

For account deletion requests, please contact:

contact@wovenex.online

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically reviewed and processed within 7 business days after successful verification of account ownership and operational requirements.

Important Notice

Account deletion may permanently remove access to delivery history, account settings, and operational information associated with the application.
