Delete Account

This page explains how users can request account deletion for Wovenex applications and services.

1. How to Request Deletion

To request account deletion, send an email to:

contact@wovenex.online

2. Required Information

  • Full Name
  • Email used in the account
  • App name (if applicable)
  • Reason (optional)

3. What Happens After Request

Once your request is received, Wovenex will review and process it within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Data Removal

All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted unless retention is required for legal reasons.

5. Important Notes

  • This action is irreversible
  • You may lose access permanently
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance
