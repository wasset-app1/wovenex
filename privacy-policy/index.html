Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Wovenex may collect, use, store, and protect information when visitors interact with our website, mobile applications, communication channels, and digital services. 1. Information We Collect

Wovenex may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, submit an inquiry, or communicate with our team through the website, mobile applications, or email. This may include your name, business name, email address, phone number, and any project or inquiry details you choose to share.

  • Contact information submitted through forms, mobile applications, or email
  • Business and project-related details you provide voluntarily
  • Basic technical or usage information generated through website or application access

2. Application Scope

This Privacy Policy also applies to mobile applications developed and published by Wovenex. Depending on the functionality of the application, we may collect additional information such as device information, location data, or user account details strictly for the purpose of providing and improving the service.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected by Wovenex may be used to respond to inquiries, communicate with potential clients, provide relevant service information, improve website and application performance, and support legitimate business operations.

  • Responding to inquiries and business requests
  • Providing service-related communication
  • Improving website, application usability, and digital presentation
  • Maintaining internal records related to business discussions

4. Location Data

Location data may be collected to enable delivery services, order tracking, and location-based features. This data is used only when necessary for the operation of the application. Location data is only collected with user permission and can be disabled at any time through device settings.

5. Application Permissions

Our applications may request certain permissions such as access to location, storage, or notifications in order to provide core functionality. These permissions are only used as required for the intended service.

6. Sharing of Information

Wovenex does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary for business operations, legal compliance, technical support, security, or service delivery, and only to the extent required for those purposes.

We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

We may retain submitted information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage business communication, maintain records, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, or support legitimate operational needs.

8. Data Protection

Wovenex applies reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, alteration, or loss. However, no website, transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights relating to the personal information you provide, including the right to request access, correction, update, or deletion of certain information, subject to legal and operational requirements.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website, mobile applications, or communications may contain references or links to third-party websites, services, or tools. Wovenex is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those third-party platforms. Users should review the policies of those third parties separately.

11. Policy Updates

Wovenex may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, operational, or business changes. Any updated version will become effective upon posting on this page unless otherwise stated.
