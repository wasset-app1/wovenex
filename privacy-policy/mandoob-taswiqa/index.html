Mandoob Taswiqa Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mandoob Taswiqa collects, uses, stores, and protects information related to delivery representatives using the application.

Information We Collect

Mandoob Taswiqa may collect information required to operate delivery services efficiently, including:

  • Name and account information.
  • Phone number and login credentials.
  • Live and background location data during delivery operations.
  • Device and application usage information.

How We Use Information

  • To assign and manage delivery orders.
  • To improve delivery tracking and customer experience.
  • To provide operational support and security.
  • To communicate important updates related to the service.
  • To send operational notifications and delivery alerts.

Location Access

The application may access precise location information in the background while the delivery representative is active or handling delivery orders. This is required to support live delivery tracking, active order management, zone validation, and operational functionality even when the application is running in the background.

Profile Photos and Identity Verification

The application may collect profile photos, identity-related images, or selfie images provided voluntarily by delivery representatives during registration, profile setup, or account updates.

These images are used to support account identification, operational verification, and profile management features.

Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

The application may use trusted third-party services such as Firebase and Google services to support notifications, application functionality, analytics, and operational reliability.
